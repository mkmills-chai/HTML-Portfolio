The Best Movies According to McKenzie

My Top 5 Movies of All Time

Pride and Prejudice (2005)

A wholesome romance with a slow burn, poetic script and a satisfying ending.

Avatar

An action packed futuristic film that transports the viewer to another world (literally), causing them to fall in love with it and its characters.

The Man From U.N.C.L.E

A riveting depiction of the Cold War era and nuanced international spy film with a sensual twist.

Interstellar

A scientifically accurate and visually stunning 10/10 film with a moving and sometimes crushing soundtrack.

Casino Royal

A sexy and thrilling spy film where the fate of the world is in the hands of one tortured yet charming man.
